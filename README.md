# 🚚 Logistics Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

- The Logistics Analytics Dashboard is an end-to-end Business Intelligence project developed using Power BI to monitor and optimize logistics operations.
- The dashboard helps logistics managers and business stakeholders track delivery performance, driver productivity, fleet utilization, customer satisfaction, and hub efficiency through interactive visualizations and KPI monitoring.
- This project demonstrates how data analytics can be used to identify operational bottlenecks, improve delivery performance, and support strategic decision-making in logistics and supply chain management.

## 🎯 Business Problem

Logistics companies manage thousands of deliveries daily across multiple hubs, drivers, and vehicles.

Without centralized reporting, it becomes difficult to:
* Monitor delivery performance
* Track driver efficiency
* Analyze fleet utilization
* Identify delayed deliveries
* Measure customer satisfaction
* Optimize hub operations

This dashboard was built to provide a unified view of logistics performance and uncover actionable insights from operational data.

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard tracks the following KPIs:

* On-Time Delivery Rate

* Delayed Deliveries

### Customer Metrics

* Customer Satisfaction Score (CSAT)
* Delivery Success Rate

### Driver Metrics

* Driver Performance Rating
* Driver Experience Analysis
* Driver Delay Contribution

### Fleet Metrics

* Active Vehicles
* Vehicles Under Maintenance
* Fleet Utilization Rate
* Vehicle Performance

### Hub Metrics

* Orders Processed per Hub
* Hub Efficiency
* Hub On-Time Delivery Rate

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Delivery Performance

* Nearly 80% of deliveries were completed on time.
* A significant portion of deliveries experienced delays, indicating opportunities for route optimization.

### Hub Efficiency

* Dallas Main Hub handled the highest order volume.
* El Paso Hub achieved the highest on-time delivery rate.

### Driver Analysis

* Experienced drivers generally maintained higher performance ratings.
* A small group of drivers contributed disproportionately to delivery delays.

### Fleet Utilization

* Approximately 73% of vehicles were actively operational.
* 27% of vehicles required maintenance, impacting delivery capacity.

### Customer Satisfaction

* Customer satisfaction remained above 84%, indicating strong service quality despite delivery challenges.

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### Improve Route Optimization

Use route analytics and traffic forecasting to reduce delivery delays.

### Predictive Vehicle Maintenance

Implement maintenance scheduling based on vehicle performance data to minimize downtime.

### Driver Performance Programs

Provide targeted training for drivers with high delay rates and reward top performers.

### Hub Capacity Planning

Redistribute workload across hubs to balance operational efficiency.

### Real-Time Monitoring

Deploy automated alerts for delivery delays and vehicle maintenance requirements.
